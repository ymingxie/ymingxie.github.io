Freestyle Roller Skating

Freestyle roller skating is a very interesting sport. I started this sport when I was 10 years old. During my undergraduate years, I served as the president of the Roller Skating Association of Zhejiang University. People say my "major" in the first two years of college was freestyle skating, because I spent almost all of my time on it. This page is to commemorate my first two years of college.
